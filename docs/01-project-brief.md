# Project Brief

## Product Name

AI-Driven Operations Intelligence Dashboard

## One-Sentence Summary

A unified, AI-augmented analytics platform that aggregates MES, ERP, and CMMS data across four plants into real-time KPI dashboards, anomaly alerts, and a natural-language query interface with chart-backed answers.

## Problem

BWI performance data lives across siloed systems. Useful insight exists, but it is slow to assemble because teams must manually gather and reconcile reports from multiple sources. That delay makes it harder to react quickly to scrap spikes, downtime trends, inventory issues, and delivery risks.

## Value Proposition

The dashboard turns fragmented operational data into one decision-making surface. Instead of waiting on manual reporting, managers get:

- live KPI visibility
- faster issue detection
- AI-assisted analysis
- less reporting overhead

## Why This Fits BWI

This project is strong for BWI because it improves the speed and quality of operational decisions without requiring a full rip-and-replace of existing systems. It creates a shared intelligence layer on top of the systems BWI already uses.

It also creates a reusable data foundation for future AI applications.

## Expected Benefits

- Real-time visibility across OEE, scrap rate, OTIF, and inventory across all plants
- Earlier issue detection, especially for scrap, downtime, and delivery risk
- Natural-language analytics that reduces report generation time from hours to seconds
- Lower manual reporting burden for engineers and managers
- Better cross-plant comparison and prioritization

## Pilot Envelope

- Estimated implementation cost: `$50,000 - $100,000`
- Estimated timeline: `2-6 months`

## Primary Users

- Plant managers
- Operations managers
- Manufacturing engineers
- Maintenance leaders
- Supply chain and planning stakeholders
- Executive leadership who need cross-plant summaries

## Key Performance Indicators

These are the business KPIs the product must make easy to monitor:

- OEE
- Scrap rate
- OTIF
- Inventory health
- Downtime trend

## Success Metrics For The Product Itself

- Time to generate a standard weekly operations report: before vs. after
- Number of issues identified via alerts before reactive discovery
- Query usage per week by management users
- Reduction in decision delays caused by unavailable or fragmented data

## North Star Goal

Move BWI from reactive, manually compiled reporting to proactive, AI-assisted operational decision-making.
